About Me

I am an Artist and Engineer with varying interests in many fields.

My technical interests include autonomous systems, robotics, data science and machine learning. I have worked as a Process Engineer, Systems Engineer and more recently a Data Scientist.

As an artist I have worked in various media including photography, sculpture, digital media and poetry.
