About

Diego Armando Plascencia Vega

Diego4rma@gmail.com

I am an Artist and Engineer with varying interests in many fields. My technical interests include feedback controls, autonomous systems, and robotics. Topics in my artwork range from the immigrant experience to the nature of representation in video media, to the built environment.
